Prakashan

Prakashan' is an in-house technical exhibition that provides first-year students with an opportunity to apply what they learn and gain confidence to aim higher. It is an initiative that not only involves building models but also informing them about the agile industry approach. This platform created by the students of JCER IEEE SB involves senior students with relevant experience to support the students in building and troubleshooting the circuits and programs.
